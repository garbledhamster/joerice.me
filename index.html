About Me

Hey there, I’m Joe Rice. I make websites and tools with AI that power your growth. My mission is to build lasting infrastructure for anyone who needs a helping hand, sharing the same tools and insights that helped me move forward in my own journey.

As of 01/16/2025, I’ve built 25 websites in just three months, harnessing AI as my primary coder. Whenever I learn new things or recall insights, I feed them into AI to bring fresh ideas to life. All of this happens alongside my full-time job, plus my favorite roles: being a dad and husband.

I’m still early in this journey, and my vision will sharpen as I learn. But one thing is certain: everything I create will be interconnected and rooted in countering negativity. Over time, this overarching vision will reveal itself—each small step is a chance to inspire others, showing that positive change and growth are always possible.

Experience

Over the past decade, I’ve grown from a curious tech enthusiast into an all-around engineer. I bring my experience in IT security, website development, and automation to every project I tackle. Along the way, I realized many people don’t see all the opportunities tech can offer—so I’ve made it my goal to help shine a light on those possibilities.

In my day job, I juggle scripting for our internal AI chatbot, writing PowerShell to streamline processes, and collaborating across industries—from local governments to manufacturing to police departments. Outside of work, I’m driven to share the same problem-solving mindset I use professionally with anyone who could benefit from it.

Balancing a full-time career, fatherhood, and a commitment to helping others keeps me on my toes, but it’s also what drives me forward. I believe in open communication and genuine collaboration, because sharing insights can create real value and spark positivity in unexpected ways.

Purpose

This platform isn’t just a portfolio—it’s a space to connect and grow. I want to lower barriers to technology by sharing tools, knowledge, and inspiration. Whether it’s a small business looking for a straightforward solution or an individual with an innovative idea, I’m here to help make it happen.

My broader vision is still unfolding, but at its core is a desire to challenge negativity and foster deeper connections. Every solution I build or idea I share is part of that collective effort. By taking each step forward, I hope to show that growth and change are not only possible—they’re within everyone’s reach.

Support
